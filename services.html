Reflexology

Reflexology is a non-invasive complementary therapy based on the principle that there are reflex points on the feet, hands, and ears that correspond to every part, gland, and organ of the body.

Who it is suitable for: Individuals of all ages looking to reduce stress, improve sleep, and support their overall wellbeing. It is particularly beneficial for those with limited mobility who prefer treatments at home.

What to expect: A deeply relaxing session where gentle pressure is applied to these reflex points. Most clients feel a profound sense of relaxation and improved wellbeing after a session.

Reiki

Reiki is a Japanese technique for stress reduction and relaxation that also promotes healing. It is administered by "laying on hands" and is based on the idea that an unseen "life force energy" flows through us.

Who it is suitable for: Anyone seeking emotional balance, stress relief, or a sense of spiritual peace. It is a very gentle therapy that can be performed seated or lying down.

What to expect: During a session, you remain fully clothed while I gently place my hands on or just above your body. You may feel sensations of warmth or tingling, and most people find it incredibly calming.

Spinal Touch Therapy

Spinal Touch is a gentle way of realigning the body's centre of gravity by using a very light touch on specific points on the back, buttocks, and abdomen.

Who it is suitable for: For those suffering from physical tension, back pain, or postural issues. It is a subtle but powerful treatment that works with the body's natural mechanics.

What to expect: A focused treatment aimed at relaxing the muscles and realigning the spine without any heavy manipulation. It is often described as feeling very light yet surprisingly effective.

End-of-Life Doula Support

As an End-of-Life Doula, I provide non-medical, holistic support and advocacy for individuals and their families during the dying process.

Who it is suitable for: Individuals facing the end of life, their families, and carers who are looking for compassionate, professional guidance and a calm presence.

What to expect: Confidential listening, help with planning and legacy work, and emotional and spiritual support. I offer a steady hand and a caring heart to ensure dignity and peace during this transition.
