A Unique Blend of Knowledge and Care

What sets my practice apart is that I bring together two seemingly different worlds. My nursing background gives me a deep understanding of the body, health and safety protocols: the grounding that allows you to relax completely. Combined with over 30 years of training in complementary natural therapies, I can offer integrated care that treats you as a whole person, not just a collection of symptoms.

This is not alternative care or conventional care; it is thoughtful, personalised care that draws on the best of both approaches.

Why You Can Trust Me

  • Enhanced DBS Certificate: I have been thoroughly vetted and cleared to work with vulnerable individuals
  • Full Professional Insurance: Your wellbeing is protected by comprehensive professional indemnity
  • Formal Qualifications: Level 5 Leadership in Health and Social Care, professional training in Reflexology, Reiki and Spinal Touch, and certification as an End-of-Life Doula
  • Real-World Experience: Eight years as a foster carer, mother of two and four decades in healthcare have taught me how to listen, adapt and truly see the person in front of me

Every session I deliver across Pembrokeshire is grounded in this foundation of safety, expertise and genuine care. When you book with The Natural Touch, you are choosing someone who understands both the science and the soul of healing.
